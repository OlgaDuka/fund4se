We are actively seeking for investment projects. We offer not just financing but "smart money". It means that besides the funding we help your project to build a network of partners, to organize your operations in the most effective way, to develop your marketing and sales activities, to obtain resources essential for the project, to prepare the company to become public.

Here are our requirements to the projects we invest in:

  • The target industry is
    • Renewable and “green” energy
    • Energy and related costs savings
    • Efficiency in energy generation
    • Facility maintenance efficiency
    • Sustainability and wellness
  • The target market
    • Exceeds
      $1 bln
    • With growth rate exceeding 10% a year
      10%
  • Your technology is
    • Cutting-edge
    • Reliable
    • Competitive one
  • You possess a
    • Proven technical process
    • Approved manufacture
    • Ability to scale a production up
  • Your consumers benefit from sufficient cost savings
  • You have an existing orders pipeline (as an advantage)

If your project fits our requirements please send us its description and we will definitely discuss investment opportunity with you.
