About
Us

Fund for Sustainability and Energy (FUND4SE) – is a Singapore-based fund (in process of VCFM license obtaining) dedicated to equity investment into companies promoting outstanding solutions based on cutting-edge technologies and having high commercial potential including high inflow of orders for procurement and deployment of such solutions.

Our target industries:

  • Renewable and “green” energy
  • Energy and related costs savings
  • Efficiency in energy generation
  • Facility maintenance efficiency
  • Sustainability and wellness

Our strategy

  • We process numerous companies at Series A2 (proven technology/ product), Series B (commercial manufacturing and sales are available) and Series C (market expansion) financing rounds. We do thoroughly conduct due diligence and select few within the best of our criteria
  • We purchase target company’s shares in amount sufficient to control its cash flows and strategic development (including manufacturing scaling-up and sales growth) and further exit through IPO or direct sale of shares (M&A)
  • We assure an essential increase in сompany’s sales volume (foremost in USA) through our wide business connections and marketing activities
  • We raise additional funding for working capital increase or for manufacturing scaling-up (if necessary)
  • We assure sufficient annual revenue growth rate and EBITDA

Our Team

Well balanced team is comprised of professionals, each possessing more than 10 years of global experience in data, marketing, financial, pricing and cost, investment and business analysis; in strategy development and implementation, planning, coordination, financial and operational management; in evaluation, deal structuring, fundraising and investment management; in business development for projects and companies in various sectors.
